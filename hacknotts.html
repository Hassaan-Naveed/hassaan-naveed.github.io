Overview

HackNotts is a yearly Hackathon at the University of Nottingham, hosted by the HackSoc Society. It lasts 24 hours and hosts a variety of activieties, such as learning about hacking or creating teams to compete in various challanges during the 24 hour period. I participated to learn about hacking and how to do it.
