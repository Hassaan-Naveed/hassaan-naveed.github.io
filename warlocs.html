Overview

The Nottingham Trent Students Union Warlocs Society focuses on tabletop gaming such as Role-Playing, Wargaming, Board games and Card games. Different sessions are held during the week for different activieties. I attend the Card and Board games session, held weekly on Thursdays from 6pm until 10pm.

Card and Board Games

The Card and Board Games night occurs every Thursday in the Students Union building at the City campus. There is a very wide selection of board games to play. I attend the board games session almost every week, from 6pm until 10pm.

Session 1

The first session I went to was an introductory taster session during Freshers week. When I arrived I joined the group of people wanting to play board games. One of the selected a board game called Dixit, a game where a player selects one card from a hand of 5. All cards have some abstract artwork on them. They then place the card face down and say a single word or phrase. The other players must choose a card from their hands that best matches the word, and place them face down. The cards are then flipped over and all players have to guess which card was the original.

After a few rounds of this, we decided to play some other games. The group split into two due to player restrictions on most other games. Our group opted to play Settlers of Catan: A strategy game where players must collect resources to build towns and cities on the board, and collect points to win the game. A single game of this lasted approximately 2.5 hours as we had to teach ourselves the rules.

Session 2

During the second session I went to we decided to play a strategy board game called Small World. This game consisted of selecting a civilisation race, and competing for resource tiles on the game board, until the player owned too many tiles and couldnt expand any further. They would then abandon their original race and choose another to begin playing, however the original would remain on the board. The game was complex with many rules and took us a fairly long time to learn, a single game lasted 2-3 hours. After this we decided to play a simpler game of Dixit for the remainder of the session.

Session 3

During the last session i went to we decided to play a strategy game called Takenoko. This game involved creating a bamboo garden by collecting and placing random tiles, and then growing and collecting bamboo pieces in order to complete various objectives, such as creating a certain formation of tiles or collecting a certain number and colour of bamboo. These objectives would then give the players points, which would be needed to win. This game lasted about 1 hour.

After this, we decided to play a Role Playing board game called Betrayal at the House on the Hill. This game involved exploring a haunted house by choosing tiles from a shuffled deck, collecting items to prepare for the main game. Eventually one player wold be selected as a traitor who would be given a scenario to read from a game book privately, while the remaining players read an equivilant scenario from a different book. These would contain details of the scenario and how the traitors mechanics would work. The remaining players would then have to complete specific objectives throughout the house, while the traitor would be trying to complete their own objectives, directly opposing the player. During the game my character died very quickly. The game lasted about 2 hours.

After this game ended we decided to play Settlers of Catan, which we had played previously. We played in pairs as we had too many people, and some wanted to leave early. I left after about 30 mins in order to get the bus.
